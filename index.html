1936-2021

Missile Man Of Pakistan

Dr.Abdul Qadeer Khan, revered as the "Mohsin-e-Pakistan" (Benefactor of Pakistan), was a towering scientific figure who revolutionized the nation’s defense. By establishing Khan Research Laboratories (KRL), he successfully spearheaded the effort to make Pakistan the first Islamic nuclear power on May 28, 1998, ensuring national sovereignty and regional balance.
